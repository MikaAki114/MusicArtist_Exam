ARIRANG by BTS

ARIRANG is the fifth full-length studio album by BTS, released on March 20, 2026. Marking the group's highly anticipated reunion after their military service hiatus, the album blends pop, hip-hop, R&B, EDM, and traditional Korean influences. Inspired by the iconic Korean folk song "Arirang," the album explores themes of identity, resilience, reunion, cultural heritage, and personal growth.
